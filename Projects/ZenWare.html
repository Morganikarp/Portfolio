ZenWare
October 2022
Unity
C#
Solo
Project Demands
  • Developing a complete product within 2 weeks.
  • Designing a game that didn't rely on a unique control scheme.
Noteworthy Contributions
  • I did all the gameplay and UI programming myself.
  • This includes making 5 unique microgames, a way to transition between them, and a scorekeeping system that runs throughout.
  • I made all art and animations myself.
Developed Skills
  • The layer mask used by the game circle gave me further practice with how masks can be used in design.
  • Syncing the background music and the gameplay allowed for some audio programming practice.
  • As each microgame existed independently, they were each a good test of robust programming.
Project Description
ZenWare was made for a 2 week-long game jam during my first year of university. It was made in Unity with C#. We were asked for games with a simple method of input, so I based my submission off of the WarioWare games. Out of the 39 submissions, ZenWare came 2nd and 3rd in the two major categories.
