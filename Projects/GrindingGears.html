Grinding Gears
Released november 2022
Unity
C#
Team
Project Demands
  • Developing a complete product within a month.
  • Designing a game for multiple players.
Noteworthy Contributions
  • I programmed the player controller, including the core mechanic.
  • I programmed the score keeping system that connects each “round”, and decides an overall winner.
  • I made the player and level art and associated animations.
Developed Skills
  • Working as part of a team and collaborating with different roles was important, as I now have a better understanding of how to integrate other works into the project.
  • Organising a team from a leader role was a new experience, and it gave me a better appreciation of the qualities needed to lead others.
Project Description
Grinding Gears was made for a month-long game jam during my first year of university. It was made in Unity with C#. We had to make a simple multiplayer game. I primarily fulfilled the role of gameplay programmer within my group. Near the end of the project, I partly embraced the producer's role as well. I also contributed some art. Out of the 23 submissions, Grinding Gears came 2nd and 5th in the two major categories.
