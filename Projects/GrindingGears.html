Grinding Gears
November 2022
Unity
C#
Team
Grinding Gears was made over the course of a month for a game jam that was part of my first year course.

I was 1 of 2 programmers in a group of 6. Despite my intended role, as the project came along I had to take on a leading role in the group, as I managed the other two programmers and the designer. I was still resonsible for the core gameplay programming, as well as the vast majority of the art. I made the movement and action system, the random stage selection, the score keeping and the win state.

While I was able to work as a team, this project taught me that sometimes you need to step up and take on more responsiblity for the sake of a project.

In the game jam it was made for, it came 5th in the entertainment catagory and 2nd in the originality catagory, out of 23 submissions.
