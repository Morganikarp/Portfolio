Exposed
June 2024
Unity
C#
Solo
Exposed was made for Interstellar Game Jam 6, which was a public game jam hosted on itch.io.

The jam was a week long, and the theme was "Isolation", so I built the game around making the player feel lost while giving them a resource to help resolve that. I used this project as an opportunity to practice shader graph within Unity. While I made a shader to darken the screen during development, it did not make it into the final game. I also practiced sound design by implementing background noise and sound effects. It was made using Unity engine and C#. The programming, design, and art were all made by me within the jam week.

In the game jam, it came in 35th place overall out of 82 submissions.
