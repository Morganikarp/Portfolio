Blackjack
December 2023
Unreal
Blueprints
Solo
Blackjack was made for my first year Coding and Scripting for Games module.

The game itself is just Blackjack. The player is given the option to stick or twist, with their resulting score updating for every card. After either busting or sticking, the dealer will then randomly draw a card and present it to the player before telling them the result of the game.

The entire game was made using Unreal Engines UI tools as well as blueprint programming. All the art was made by me.

The game and the programming practices I used during the development of it earned me 82% for the module coursework mark.
