Project Demands
  • Developing a complete product within a week.

Noteworthy Contributions
  • I did the majority of the system programming, as well as all the UI and file manipulation programming.
  • I contributed to the gameplay programming, specifically the player controller.
  • I made some UI art.
  • I started managing and coordinating my teammates' later into the project, as we were lacking leadership.
Developed Skills
  • Being a team project, integrating the work of dedicated artists into my work as a programmer is an important skill to develop.
  • Being a team leader is a rare experience, and so I learned some more about how I should organise and appoint work to a team of specialists.
Project Description
Whole Horse was made for the week-long 2024 Global Game Jam. It was made in Unity with C#. The game only had to follow the theme of “Make Me Laugh”, so my team decided to make a multiplayer puzzle game with a goofy aesthetic. On our team of 9, I was primarily a UI and systems programmer, but I took on a producer role later into the project. I also did some UI art.
