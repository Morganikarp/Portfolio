Fishsplosion
April 2024
Unity
C#
Solo
Fishsplosion was made for FishFest, which was a weeklong public game jam hosted on Itch.io.

While not my best work, I am happy with a few things from this project. The aspect that I believe turned out the best was the art. While I took longer on it than I should, I think it all fits the intended style well. The best thing I learnt from this project was better time management. I made this game in a pretty mismanaged way, and I believe I have gotten better since this project.

It was made using Unity engine and C#. The programming, design, and art were all made by me within the jam week.

In the game jam, it came 344th overall out of 562 submissions.
